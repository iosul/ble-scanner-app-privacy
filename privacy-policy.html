Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Introduction

Sensovium Technology Pvt Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") operates the mobile application BLE Scanner – Find Devices (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Bluetooth Scan Data

The App scans for nearby Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices to display their names, MAC addresses, signal strength (RSSI), and other publicly broadcasted metadata. This data is processed in real-time only and is not stored, transmitted, or retained by us or the App after the scan session ends.

Location Data

On newer versions of iOS and Android, Bluetooth scanning requires location permissions. If you grant this permission, your device's location is used solely to enable Bluetooth scanning functionality. We do not collect, store, or transmit your location data.

Device Information

Basic non-identifying device information (such as device model and operating system version) may be accessed locally by the App to ensure compatibility and proper functionality. This information is not collected or transmitted by us.

What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number)
  • Account information (the App does not require user accounts)
  • Precise or coarse location data
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Crash logs or diagnostic data
  • Advertising identifiers

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Data Storage and Security

All Bluetooth scan data is ephemeral and processed locally on your device in real-time. No data is stored on your device or transmitted to external servers. Once you close the App or stop scanning, the data is no longer accessible.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy at the URL provided in the App Store listing. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
